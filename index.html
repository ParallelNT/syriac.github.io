About



T he Syriac Bible has a long and notable history. It's origins (both the Syriac Old and New Testament) are cloaked in obscurity.

This website is dedicated to just one part of the Syriac Bible: the study of the Syriac New Testament and English language resources dedicated to accomplish that.

The aim is both general in its scope and scholarly in its orientation. The study of the English Parallel Syriac New Testament is a good starting point. All the content on this website was written with the layman in mind.

As you may know, the study of the Syriac New Testament in English is only in its infancy. You have plenty of choices on the internet and I appreciate the time you spend with us.

I hope you enjoy the content and if we deserve it, please consider sharing.
