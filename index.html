About



The Syriac Bible has a long and notable history. It's origins (both the Syriac Old and New Testament) are cloaked in obscurity.

This website is dedicated to just one part of the Syriac Bible: the study of the Syriac New Testament and English language resources.



Whats New?


The motivation and the genesis of this website grew out of two things:

Frustration with the lack of English resources for the study of Syriac, and
Superficial nature of a vast majority of the preexisting reousrces.

Included here are English language resources which explain to the layman what the text says. The most basic place to start is the Syriac Bible.

That is why, I have brought together the Syriac New Testament translations of J.W. Etheridge, James Murdock and George Lamsa and combined them into one book, The Syriac Parallel New Testament.

These three English translations from the Syriac text are time tested, straightforward and easy to understand.
